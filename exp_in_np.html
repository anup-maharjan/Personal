My Job Experience Story in Nepal

Eden Garden Secondary Boarding School
I started my first job at Eden Garden Secondary Boarding School as a Computer Teacher and Computer Lab Operator in 2009, where I got to know about the real-time scenario of different aspects of Computer Systems and their networking.
Rapid Web Solutions Pvt. Ltd
Then, I continually worked hard to complete a Bachelor's degree at that time, so; I joined an IT Company named Rapid Web Solutions Pvt. Ltd, from February 2014 to June 2020. The company provided me with the opportunity to enhance my knowledge regarding Information Technology and Front-end web development, where I learn to create websites from scratch and process PSD2HTML conversion. The company gave me a great boost and encouragement.
Premier International School
I again got an opportunity to work at another IB school, “Premier International School”. This time I already had developed good knowledge in the field of Computer Hardware and Software technologies. I worked as a Computer Faculty from August 2016 to April 2020 where I got an opportunity to teach following the IB curriculum. I got an opportunity to broaden my knowledge of IB Philosophy, which is very much essential for growth mindset learners to recognize as global citizens.
Aptech Computer Education || Sprout Technology (Cloud Factory)
Furthermore, I had also worked as a Faculty member at Aptech Computer Education and in parallel, as a data-entry employee at Sprout Technology (Cloud Factory).
Sanskriti International School
Lastly, I worked as ICT Teacher at Sanskriti International School in 2021 where I got further opportunities to enhance professional development growth.
